About Me

I received my Ph.D. in Finance from Nanyang Technological University in 2021. I subsequently joined the Department of Accounting, School of Management, Jinan University. Since 2024, I have also been serving as Deputy Director of the MPAcc Center at the School of Management and Assistant Head of the Department of Accounting, roles that keep me both busy and engaged across research, teaching, and service. My research sits at the intersection of empirical corporate finance, information disclosure, cybersecurity risk, and financial analysts, broadly driven by a curiosity about how information (and sometimes misinformation) shapes corporate decisions. In my life, I try to follow a simple philosophy: do good and do well, the idea that shapes how I approach my work and daily interactions. Ultimately, I hope my work, whether in research, teaching, or service, contributes in small but meaningful ways to both academic knowledge and the broader community.

关于我

我于 2021 年获得南洋理工大学金融学博士学位，毕业后加入暨南大学管理学院会计学系，任助理教授，2024年起担任管理学院 MPAcc 中心副主任及会计学系主任助理。 我的研究主要在实证公司金融、信息披露、网络安全风险以及金融分析师等领域，核心关注点在于信息（有时也包括错误信息）如何影响企业决策。 在生活中，我努力践行一个简单的理念：do good and do well。 无论是在学术研究、教育教学还是学术服务方面，我都希望自己的工作能够以微小但有意义的方式，为学术知识的发展以及更广泛的社会贡献力量。
