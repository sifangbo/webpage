About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Accounting at Jinan University, where I also serve as Deputy Director of the MPAcc Center at the School of Management and Assistant Head of the Department of Accounting —roles that keep me both busy and engaged across research, teaching, and academic service. I received my Ph.D. in Finance from Nanyang Technological University in 2021. My research sits at the intersection of empirical corporate finance, corporate governance, cybersecurity risk, and financial analysts, broadly driven by a curiosity about how information (and sometimes misinformation) shapes corporate decisions. I enjoy teaching and genuinely appreciate the classroom as a place where ideas are tested, challenged, and occasionally made less confusing—for both students and myself. In both my research and personal life, I try to follow a simple philosophy: do good and do well—the idea that shapes how I approach my work and daily interactions. Ultimately, I hope my work—whether in research, teaching, or service—contributes in small but meaningful ways to both academic knowledge and the broader community. [Download CV]
